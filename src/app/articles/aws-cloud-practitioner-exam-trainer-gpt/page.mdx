I built a custom GPT to help people prepare for the **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner** exam. It uses real-world questions and answers, and the idea is simple: if you can score above 90% three times in a row, you are ready to pass the exam.

## What it does

The [Cloud Exam Trainer](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-67a5e0b1237081918639fe5eedde7d79-cloud-exam-trainer) GPT:

- Asks **real-world exam-style questions** covering all Cloud Practitioner domains
- Gives **detailed explanations** for each answer
- Tracks your progress and adapts to weak areas
- Uses a **pass guarantee**: score 90%+ three times in a row and you are ready for the real exam

## How to use it

1. Open the [Cloud Exam Trainer](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-67a5e0b1237081918639fe5eedde7d79-cloud-exam-trainer) (requires ChatGPT Plus).
2. Start a new chat and ask for a practice session.
3. Answer the questions one by one — the GPT will give you feedback after each answer.
4. Keep going until you hit 90%+ three sessions in a row.

## Tip: Get questions one at a time

To avoid overload, ask the GPT to **send questions one by one**. For example:

- *"Give me one question at a time. Wait for my answer before giving the next one."*
- *"Quiz me one question at a time. Explain the answer after I respond."*

This keeps the flow focused and makes it easier to learn from each explanation before moving on.

## Why I built it

I passed the AWS Solutions Architect Associate exam and know how useful targeted practice is. The Cloud Practitioner is an entry-level cert, but the official materials can feel dry. This GPT gives you exam-style practice with immediate feedback, so you can study more efficiently.
